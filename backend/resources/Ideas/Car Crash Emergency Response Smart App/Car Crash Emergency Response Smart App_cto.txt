10. **Team Leadership and Collaboration**: Strong leadership skills to build and motivate cross-functional teams of developers, engineers, designers, and quality assurance testers, fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

11. **Ethical and Responsible Technology Development**: Commitment to ethical and responsible technology development practices, with a focus on user safety, privacy, and inclusivity, and adherence to ethical guidelines and industry standards in the development and deployment of the app.

12. **Passion for Public Safety and Impact**: Genuine passion for leveraging technology to improve public safety and emergency response systems, driving a personal commitment to excellence and impact within the team and the broader community.
